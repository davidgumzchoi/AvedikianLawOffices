Matt specializes in handling all aspects of the firm’s wills, trusts and estates practice, including basic and complex planning for small to mid-sized estates, probates and trust administration. In addition, Matt has a detailed understanding of California’s long-term care Medi-Cal program and assists clients with planning for the eventuality of facing significant long-term care costs, as well as managing and preserving assets to this effect. Matt advises clients on every stage of the Medi-Cal process: from initial eligibility through protecting against eventual recovery claims or “Medi-Cal liens.” This includes administrative appeals, court proceedings, share of cost determinations, annual Medi-Cal eligibility redeterminations, and balancing long-term care needs with important estate, gift, income and property tax considerations. Matt also represents clients on similar matters as a member of Hanson O’Toole, LLP, a local estate planning and elder law firm located in Pleasant Hill, California.

Prior to founding Avedikian Law Offices, Matt practiced at the Walnut Creek office of a major East Bay firm handling general business and corporate law, real estate transactions and leasing, and nonprofit formation and governance. Matt began his career counseling entrepreneurs and business owners on business planning and transactions, commercial leasing projects and land acquisitions in a leading Central Valley firm. Matt also developed a practice niche in the law of cooperatives early in his career. As a result, Matt understands the tax, antitrust and business aspects of cooperative organizations and has extensive experience representing and forming agricultural and fish marketing co-ops situated throughout California.

During law school, Matt clerked on two occasions for The Honorable James A. Ardaiz (Ret.), former Presiding Justice of the California Court of Appeal, Fifth District.
